More About us

Welcome to We Got Your Events, where your vision meets our expertise, and together we create unforgettable experiences.

Our journey began in the vibrant corridors of Abeda Inamdar Senior College, where two friends, Alfiya Shaikh and Mohd. Amaan Shaikh, discovered a shared passion for bringing people together through meticulously planned and beautifully executed events. What started as organizing college festivals and informal gatherings quickly blossomed into a full-fledged professional venture.

At We Got Your Events, we believe that every event, big or small, is a unique opportunity to create lasting memories. Our dynamic team brings creativity, dedication, and a keen eye for detail to every project we undertake. From corporate conferences and weddings to themed parties and charitable fundraisers, we tailor each event to reflect your distinct style and needs.
